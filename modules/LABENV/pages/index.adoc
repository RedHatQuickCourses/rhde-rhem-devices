= Lab Environment

== Instructions to Launch Your Lab on the Red Hat Online Learning (ROLE) Platform

. Log in to the xref:#ROLE-Portal-Links[ROLE platform]
. Search for the catalog entry: `HOL019`.
. Click the menu:Launch[] button for the catalog entry in the search results.
. On the Course page, click the menu:Lab Environment[] tab.
. Click the menu:Create[] button on the Lab Environment page to launch the lab.
. Wait for the lab creation process to complete.
. When the lab is created, refer to the menu:SSH Private Key & Instructions[] on the page to access your lab.
